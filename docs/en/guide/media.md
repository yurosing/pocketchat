# Photos & stickers

## Send a photo

Copy an image (a screenshot, for example) and, with a chat open, press
<kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>V</kbd> — it uploads and gets sent to your contact.

::: tip If a photo won't send
Try the `/pm hosts` command — it shows which upload sites are currently
reachable from your connection. More in
[Where files get uploaded](/en/config/hosts).
:::

## Your own stickers and GIFs

Find the `pmchat-stickers` folder (see [Where the mod's files live](/en/reference/folders))
and drop your own `.png` and `.gif` files in there. They show up right away in
the sticker picker, ready to send — even animated GIFs.

<Shot src="/img/stickers.png" caption="The sticker and GIF picker." />

Recently sent stickers float to the top of the list automatically.

## Chat wallpaper

Put your own picture into the `pmchat-wallpapers` folder and pick it in
settings. More in [Appearance](/en/config/appearance).
